Meet the Team

James Feore

Technical Program Manager, Wussfinder.com

After revolutionizing the world of bullying in 2015 with Wussfinder.com, James Feore went on to pioneer new and exciting ways to "bring about disruptive change, in order to make the world a worse place for everyone." He quickly discovered the bullying potential of even simple games, and began to explore the medium. As part of the team at Four in a Row, James constantly strives to bring a voice to the often under-represented "arrogant jerk" programming community.

Bret Ladenburg

Senior Program Manager, Yourtube.com

Bret Ladenburg has been finding patterns of four ever since the fateful day when of course, he turned four years old. Born into a family of avid Four In A Row players, he has been playing and honing his skills for over 22 years until recently, when he was approached by 3 young game enthusiasts to work on a project with them. This of course became the game you see before you today. Now, Bret uses this new game as both entertainment, and tool, to find the best Four In A Row players out there and recruit them into his new clan where all Four In A Row enthusiasts can come rejoice.
